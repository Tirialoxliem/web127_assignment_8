Philadelphia

Next up is the city of brotherly love, Philadelphia. Philadelphia is the 7th most populated metropolitan area in the United States. Many people refer to Philadelphia as Philly. When you visit Philadelphia do not forget to pick yourself up their famous Philly cheesesteak from Pat's or Geno's. Philadelphia has a very rich history. There are many places to visit for fun and entertainment, such as seeing the Liberty Bell, looking at Independence Hall where the United States Declarations of Independence was signed, Eastern State Penitentiary, the statue of Rocky at the Philadelphia Museum of Art, and at Philadelphia's Waterfront. Now for the last city that we will be looking at is San Francisco.

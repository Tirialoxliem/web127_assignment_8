San Francisco

If you are planning a trip to the west coast, there is no better place to visit than San Francisco. The San Francisco metropolitan area's population is the 13th most populated area in the United States. One of the best features of San Francisco is the weather, it stays a comfortable temperature all year round. There are many places to visit and enjoy in San Francisco as well. You can check out the Golden Gate Bridge, Fisherman's Wharf, Alcatraz Island, Golden Gate Park, Twin Peaks, Lombard Street, and the Palace of Fine Arts. Be prepared to be going up many hills though.

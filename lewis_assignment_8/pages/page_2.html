New York

Let us begin by learning a little bit about the most populated city in the United States, New York City. People from all over the world flock to New York City all year round. Some even consider New York City as the capital of the world. There are so many things to do and see in New York City. Some of the most famous stops in New York City include the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, Ground Zero, Yankee Stadium, Central Park, and Times Square. There are five boroughs of New York City which are Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, The Bronx, and Staten Island. So, do not miss out on a fun experience in New York City.

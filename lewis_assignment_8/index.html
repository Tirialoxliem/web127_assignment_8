US Cities

The United States is made up of 3.5 million square miles. These square miles are made up of urban, suburban, and rural areas. On our site we are going to talk about the urban areas known as cities. In the United States there are 384 metropolitan statistical areas. There is the New York metropolitan area with almost 20 million in its population which is the most populated metropolitan area in the United States, all the way to the smallest metropolitan area of Carson City, Nevada which has a population of almost 60 thousand. These next few pages you will learn a little about some of the most populated cities in the United States.
